ⓘ What do these columns mean? (tap to expand)
Students — active students assigned to the coach this month.
Revenue — fees actually collected from this coach's students this month.
Pending — fees not yet collected (students still due) this month.
Salary Cost — the fixed salary you pay this coach per month.
Net Profit — Revenue − Salary Cost (real profit from this coach right now).
Potential Net — what Net Profit becomes if every assigned student pays.
ROI / Pot.ROI — how many times the coach earns back their salary, shown as a multiplier: e.g. 3.5× means for every ₹1 of salary the coach brings in ₹3.5. "now" = from collected fees, "max" = if all students pay. Below 1× = the coach costs more than they bring in.
